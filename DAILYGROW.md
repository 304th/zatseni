## Product

Multi-service marketing automation platform. Reviews module is one of several products alongside analytics, ads auditing, and CRM tools.

### Product Suite
1. **Review Management** — aggregate, respond, collect reviews
2. **Analytics** — cross-channel marketing analytics
3. **Ads Audit** — Yandex Direct, VK quality checks (73 parameters)
4. **DailyBot** — WhatsApp/Telegram automation
5. **Widgets** — review widgets, popups, chat buttons

### Review Service Features
- **Aggregation:** Yandex Maps, 2GIS, Google Maps, ProDoctorov, Avito
- **AI Responses:** ChatGPT-powered auto-reply suggestions
- **Collection:** QR codes + WhatsApp/Telegram messages
- **Negative capture:** Routes bad reviews to support, not public
- **Multi-location:** Dashboard for managing multiple branches
- **Review widget:** Embed reviews with photos on website

## Strengths

1. **Multi-product platform** — stickier, more value per customer
2. **AI responses** — ChatGPT-powered, saves time
3. **Aggregation focus** — monitors existing reviews, not just collection
4. **Analytics integration** — ties reviews to marketing performance
5. **14-day trial** — low barrier to try
6. **Cheap entry** — 960₽/mo annual is competitive

## Weaknesses

1. **WhatsApp dependency** — blocked/unstable in Russia
2. **Complexity** — many products, may confuse SMBs
3. **Concerning financials** — 0₽ revenue in 2021, liquidation notice
4. **1-person company** — support/reliability risk
5. **No SMS** — relies on messaging apps for outreach
6. **Киров-based** — perceived as smaller operation vs Moscow

## Positioning

DailyGrow is **monitoring-first + AI responses**, not collection-first.

- Primary value: Aggregate reviews, respond with AI
- Secondary: Collect new reviews via QR/messages
- Tertiary: Analytics, ads audit, CRM tools

**Different from Revvy/Say-Say** which are collection-first.

## Target Market Overlap

**DailyGrow targets:**
- Businesses with existing review problem (need aggregation)
- Multi-location chains
- Marketing-savvy customers who want analytics bundle

**We target:**
- Businesses who need MORE reviews
- Single-location SMBs
- Non-technical owners who want simple solution

**Overlap:** Medium — some customers want both, most want one or other.
