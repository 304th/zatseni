## Product

Gamified review collection service for local businesses. Customers complete game-like surveys and leave reviews on Yandex Maps, 2GIS, Google Maps, Avito, Zoon, Prodoctorov.

### Key Features
- **Gamification:** Interactive surveys instead of boring forms
- **Negative capture:** Unhappy customers write to private form, not public
- **CRM integrations:** YCLIENTS, amoCRM, Bitrix24, iiko, dental software
- **Messaging:** WhatsApp, Telegram, VK
- **QR codes:** Free cards for offline collection
- **Manager ratings:** Track employee performance by customer feedback

## Strengths

1. **Gamification angle** — unique differentiator, higher engagement
2. **Cheap entry** — 0₽ for QR, 1,290₽ basic tier
3. **CRM integrations** — broad coverage (Bitrix, amoCRM, YCLIENTS)
4. **Negative capture** — prevents public bad reviews
5. **Employee ratings** — value-add for management

## Weaknesses

1. **WhatsApp dependency** — blocked/unstable in Russia (Dec 2025)
2. **No SMS** — relies on messaging apps
3. **Young company** — ИП registered June 2025, limited track record
4. **Small scale** — 193 Bitrix24 installs is modest
5. **No public financials** — hard to verify stability
