# Otzovik.ai — Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Acquire 100 paying customers in 6 months, 500 in 12 months.

**Budget:** 50-150k₽/month depending on phase.

**Primary channels:** Yandex Direct, SEO, Telegram, partnerships.

**Key message:** "SMS работает. WhatsApp — нет. Ваши клиенты получат сообщение."

---

## 1. Target Audience

### Primary Segments (Priority Order)

| Segment | Size (Russia) | Why Target | Pain Points |
|---------|---------------|------------|-------------|
| **Beauty salons** | ~150k | High visit frequency, tech-savvy, use YCLIENTS | Low ratings hurt bookings, competitors steal clients |
| **Cafes/restaurants** | ~100k | Care about ratings, use iiko/Poster | Negative reviews kill foot traffic |
| **Auto services** | ~80k | High ticket, loyal customers | Trust is everything, 1 bad review = lost customer |
| **Medical clinics** | ~50k | ProDoctorov + Yandex ratings critical | Patients research heavily before booking |
| **Fitness/yoga** | ~30k | Monthly subscriptions, community-driven | Word of mouth matters, ratings = new members |

### Decision Maker Profile

- **Title:** Owner, marketing manager, administrator
- **Age:** 28-50
- **Tech level:** Uses smartphone, comfortable with dashboards
- **Budget authority:** Can approve 1-5k₽/month without escalation
- **Motivation:** More customers, less time on reputation management

### Buying Triggers

1. Received a bad review recently
2. Competitor has higher rating
3. New location opening (need reviews fast)
4. Slow season (need to boost visibility)
5. Saw competitor using similar tool

## 2. Positioning & Messaging

### Core Value Proposition

> **Otzovik.ai — сбор отзывов для Яндекс Карт и 2ГИС. От 1,190₽/месяц.**

### Key Messages by Audience

| Audience | Message | Why It Works |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Cold** | "Получайте больше положительных отзывов на автопилоте" | Benefit-focused, no jargon |
| **Aware of problem** | "Перехватывайте негатив до публикации" | Addresses fear of bad reviews |
| **Comparing options** | "SMS работает везде. WhatsApp заблокирован" | Differentiator vs Revvy |
| **Price-sensitive** | "Дешевле Revvy в 3 раза. Результат тот же" | Direct competitor attack |

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "SMS дорого" | "9₽/SMS vs потерянный клиент из-за плохого отзыва" |
| "Клиенты не любят SMS" | "98% открываемость. Email — 20%. Выбор очевиден" |
| "У нас уже хороший рейтинг" | "4.5 → 4.8 = +15% новых клиентов по исследованиям" |
| "Мы пробовали, не работает" | "Через WhatsApp? Он заблокирован. SMS дойдёт" |

## 3. Marketing Channels

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)

**Budget:** 30-50k₽/month

#### SEO (Long-term, Free Traffic)

**Target keywords:**
- "сбор отзывов яндекс карты" (500 searches/mo)
- "как получить отзывы 2гис" (300 searches/mo)
- "сервис сбора отзывов" (200 searches/mo)
- "отзывы для бизнеса" (400 searches/mo)
- "revvy альтернатива" (50 searches/mo)

**Actions:**
1. Create landing pages for each segment (beauty, cafe, auto, etc.)
2. Write 5-10 blog posts:
   - "Как поднять рейтинг на Яндекс Картах в 2025"
   - "WhatsApp заблокирован: что делать бизнесу"
   - "Сравнение сервисов сбора отзывов"
   - "Как отвечать на негативные отзывы"
3. Get listed on review aggregators (crmindex.ru, picktech.ru)

**Cost:** 0₽ (time investment) or 20-30k₽ (copywriter)

**Expected results:** 3-6 months to see organic traffic, 100-500 visits/month after 6 months

#### Website Optimization

- Landing page A/B testing (headline, CTA, pricing display)
- Add trust signals: customer logos, review count, security badges
- Live chat (Carrot Quest or similar) — 0-2k₽/month
- Exit-intent popup with trial offer

### Phase 2: Paid Acquisition (Month 2-4)

**Budget:** 50-100k₽/month

#### Yandex Direct (Primary Paid Channel)

**Campaign structure:**

| Campaign | Keywords | Budget | Expected CPC | Expected CAC |
|----------|----------|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Brand | "otzovik.ai", "отзовик" | 5k₽ | 10-20₽ | 500₽ |
| Competitors | "revvy", "getreview", "saysay" | 15k₽ | 30-50₽ | 1,500₽ |
| Problem | "плохие отзывы", "как удалить отзыв" | 20k₽ | 20-40₽ | 1,000₽ |
| Solution | "сбор отзывов", "отзывы яндекс карты" | 30k₽ | 25-45₽ | 1,200₽ |
| Retargeting | Site visitors | 10k₽ | 5-15₽ | 600₽ |

**Ad copy examples:**

```
Headline: Отзывы для Яндекс Карт — от 1,190₽/мес
Text: SMS-рассылка клиентам после визита. Негатив остаётся приватным. 14 дней бесплатно.
CTA: Попробовать бесплатно
```

```
Headline: WhatsApp заблокирован. SMS работает
Text: Revvy использует WhatsApp — сообщения не доходят. Мы отправляем SMS — 100% доставка.
CTA: Перейти на Otzovik.ai
```

**Optimization:**
- Start with manual bidding, switch to auto after 100 conversions
- Negative keywords: "бесплатно", "скачать", "отзывы о компании [name]"
- Dayparting: 9:00-21:00 (business hours)
- Geo: Moscow, SPb first, then expand

#### VK Ads (Secondary)

**Targeting:**
- Business owners groups
- YCLIENTS, iiko, Poster users (interest targeting)
- Lookalike from customer list

**Budget:** 15-20k₽/month

**Expected CAC:** 1,500-2,500₽ (higher than Yandex, but good for awareness)

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 4-6+)

**Budget:** 100-150k₽/month

#### Telegram Marketing

**Channels to target:**
- Business/marketing channels (paid posts): 5-15k₽ per post
- Owner communities: "Рестораторы", "Бьюти бизнес", "Автосервис"
- Create own channel with tips on reputation management

**Telegram Ads:**
- Target business-related channels
- Cost: 2-5₽ per view, need 50k+ views for effect
- Budget: 20-30k₽/month

#### Content Marketing

**YouTube:**
- "Как мы подняли рейтинг с 4.2 до 4.8 за месяц" (case study)
- Tutorial videos for setup
- Cost: 10-20k₽ per video (if outsourced)

**Guest posts:**
- vc.ru articles (free, need quality content)
- Habr (if technical angle)
- Industry blogs

#### Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Existing customer refers new customer
- Both get 1 month free or 500 bonus SMS
- Track via unique referral codes

**Cost:** ~500₽ per referral (1 month Старт value)

**Expected:** 10-20% of new customers from referrals after 6 months

|---------|------|-------|----------|

| **iiko** | Integration + co-marketing | Restaurants trust them | Partner program, webinars |

2. Apply for marketplace listing (free exposure)
3. Propose co-marketing (webinar, email to their users)
4. Revenue share if they push our product

### Agency Partnerships

**Target:** Marketing agencies serving local businesses

**Offer:**
- White-label option (future)
- 20% revenue share for referred clients
- Dedicated partner dashboard

**Outreach:**
- Find agencies on vc.ru, Telegram
- Cold email with case study
- Offer free pilot for their clients

## 8. Competitive Response

### If Revvy Lowers Prices
- Emphasize reliability (SMS vs blocked WhatsApp)
- Highlight included SMS in our pricing
- Don't race to bottom — compete on value

### If New Competitor Enters
- Move fast on partnerships (lock in CRM integrations)
- Build switching costs (data, integrations)
- Stay cheaper than incumbents

### If WhatsApp Gets Unblocked
- Unlikely in near term
- Add WhatsApp as option, keep SMS as default
- Position as "multi-channel" — works everywhere
