# Competitor Analysis

## Market Overview

Russian review management market is growing as local businesses recognize the importance of Yandex Maps and 2GIS ratings. Key players focus on aggregation, automated responses, and review generation.

## Main Competitors

### 1. Revvy.ai (Market Leader)

**Pricing:** 2,990 - 4,990 RUB/month
- Standard: 3,990 RUB/mo - review collection, WhatsApp messaging
- Extended: 4,990 RUB/mo - + marketing templates, dedicated manager

**Strengths:**
- First mover, strong brand recognition
- WhatsApp integration (higher engagement than SMS)
- Deep CRM integrations (YCLIENTS, amoCRM, Bitrix24, iiko)
- Multi-platform (Yandex, Google, 2GIS, TripAdvisor, Zoon)
- Proven results: users report 4-star to 4.5-star improvements

**Weaknesses:**
- Expensive entry point - 2,990 RUB minimum vs our 990 RUB
- No free trial mentioned - high barrier to try
- Complex feature set - overwhelming for small businesses
- WhatsApp dependency - requires business WhatsApp API setup
- No SMS option - WhatsApp-only limits reach to older demographics

**Counter Strategy:**
- Position as simpler and cheaper alternative
- SMS-first approach (works without smartphone apps)
- 14-day free trial with 50 SMS
- One-click setup vs complex WhatsApp integration

### 2. DailyGrow.ru

**Strengths:**
- AI-powered auto-responses (ChatGPT)
- Review widget for websites
- API access for developers
- Multi-platform aggregation

**Weaknesses:**
- Focus on response, not generation - reactive vs proactive
- No SMS/WhatsApp outreach - waits for reviews to appear
- Technical setup required - API/widget integration
- No CRM integrations listed - manual workflow

**Counter Strategy:**
- Emphasize proactive review generation vs passive monitoring
- Highlight "get reviews before they go public" angle
- Simpler non-technical setup

### 3. Repometr

**Pricing:** ~2,500 RUB/mo for 8 locations

**Strengths:**
- 14 platforms supported
- Branch/location management
- Consolidated dashboard

**Weaknesses:**
- Aggregation focus - monitors existing reviews
- No outbound messaging - does not generate new reviews
- Enterprise-oriented - complex for small business

**Counter Strategy:**
- "We generate reviews, they just watch them"
- Simple per-location pricing

### 4. RocketData

**Pricing:** Enterprise (10,000+ RUB/mo estimated)

**Strengths:**
- Comprehensive ORM (Online Reputation Management)
- AI auto-responses
- Multi-location management

**Weaknesses:**
- Enterprise pricing - out of reach for SMBs
- Overkill features - local cafe does not need ORM suite
- Long sales cycle - demo required, no self-serve

**Counter Strategy:**
- Self-serve signup in 2 minutes
- Transparent pricing on website
- No sales calls required

### 5. Pointer (pntr.io)

**Strengths:**
- 50+ platforms
- Review monitoring

**Weaknesses:**
- Monitoring only - no proactive outreach
- No pricing transparency
- Generic solution - not Russia-specific

**Counter Strategy:**
- Russia-first (Yandex + 2GIS priority)
- SMS outreach included

### 6. Otzyvy Pro (2GIS native)

**Strengths:**
- Native 2GIS integration
- Free tier available

**Weaknesses:**
- 2GIS only - no Yandex Maps
- Limited features - basic monitoring
- No outbound messaging

**Counter Strategy:**
- Both Yandex AND 2GIS in one
- Active review generation

## Our Differentiation

### 1. Price Leader
- 990 RUB vs 2,990 RUB = 3x cheaper entry
- Transparent pricing, no hidden fees
- SMS packs for pay-as-you-go

### 2. Simplicity
- Sign up, Add business, Send SMS (3 steps)
- No WhatsApp Business API setup

### 3. SMS-First

## Weaknesses to Address

### Our Current Gaps:

1. **No WhatsApp** - Higher engagement, but complex setup

   - Future: Add as option, but not priority
